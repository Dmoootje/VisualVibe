## Safeguards and factual parity

- SEO starts with search intent, sitemap and page purpose before development.
- WCAG 2.2 is referenced without making a universal legal-conformance claim.
- Good Core Web Vitals do not guarantee top rankings.
- The Belgian legal and privacy section is explicitly a working checklist, not legal advice.
- Non-essential tracking must genuinely wait for valid consent; a decorative banner alone is insufficient.
- A sitemap does not guarantee crawling or indexing, and `noindex` is not a security measure.
- Redirects point to the most relevant replacement rather than automatically to the homepage.
- Domain and account control, handover, backups, maintenance, monitoring and post-launch review all remain explicit.
