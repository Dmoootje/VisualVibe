## Claim verification

- Google confirms that ordinary SEO foundations apply to AI Overviews and AI Mode without special technical requirements.
- Google primarily composes snippets from page content and may use a meta description when suitable.
- Google stopped showing FAQ rich results on 7 May 2026 and subsequently removed its FAQ rich-result documentation.

- Bing's AI Performance public preview reports citations, cited pages and sampled grounding queries, while explicitly warning that aggregate data does not establish ranking, authority or a page's role in an answer.

The article keeps appropriate caveats: no guaranteed snippet or AI mention, no universal AEO score, and no inference of authority, causality or business value from citations or referrals alone.
