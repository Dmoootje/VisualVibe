## Safeguards retained

- The tour is a snapshot, not a guarantee of the current setup, availability, price, menu or package.
- Platform and subscription-plan features may change.
- A marketing tour is not a certified measurement record or formal accessibility assessment.
- Privacy is planned before capture; blurring is only a fallback.
- GDPR roles, legal basis, processors, cookies, retention and international transfers require situation-specific review.
- Essential information and booking actions remain available through accessible text, images and contact alternatives.
- Hosting, ownership, recurring costs, exports, updates and recapture triggers require explicit responsibility.
- Visits and interactions do not prove causation or additional bookings.
