# Podcast-to-social English localisation review

## Scope and outcome

Reviewed the complete Dutch source, English translation and localisation brief. Approved after route corrections.

## Parity and editorial quality

- Source: 3,498 words; translation: 3,225 words.
- All 16 H2 sections are preserved.
- Complete component parity: 1 LeadIntro, 2 QuoteBlocks, 1 FeatureGrid, 2 ComparisonTables, 1 RoadmapBlock, 1 NoticeBox, 1 ChecklistBlock, 1 DoDontGrid, 1 FaqAccordion and 1 BlogCTA.
- All 8 FAQ entries preserve both the detailed and plain-answer intent.
- The English reads as concise professional editorial guidance rather than a literal sentence-by-sentence rendering.

## Safeguards retained

- Clips must work independently and may need context restored; editing must not strengthen a claim by removing nuance.
- Automatic captions require manual correction for names, figures, dialect and specialist terms; full transcripts and clip captions serve different accessibility needs.
- Consent for a complete episode does not automatically cover standalone extracts, advertising or a new context.
- Music, photographs, screen recordings, charts and contributed footage may have separate rights and licence conditions.
- Channel availability, duration and display can change; the workflow remains independent of one platform feature.
- Views are treated as a distribution signal, not proof of understanding, trust, leads, revenue or causality.
- Content ownership, approval status, consent scope, source timestamps, claims and expiry dates remain part of the publication workflow.
