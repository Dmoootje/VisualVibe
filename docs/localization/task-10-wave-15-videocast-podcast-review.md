# Videocast versus podcast English localisation review

Independently reviewed and approved after correcting the canonical parent-pillar and author identity paths.

- 3,462 Dutch versus 3,784 English words; 17/17 H2 sections and 8/8 FAQs with plain answers.
- Exact component parity across the comparison, workflow, checklist, notice and decision grid.
- Format follows audience context, content purpose and distribution rather than a claim that video is inherently better.
- Captions and transcripts retain distinct accessibility and reuse functions.
- Guest consent, music, imagery, clips, advertising and derivative-use rights remain explicit.
- Cost covers preparation, recording, editing, versions, accessibility and distribution, not equipment alone.
- Cross-platform metrics require compatible definitions and do not prove reach or business causation.
- Routes, canonical relationships, metadata and localisation brief are aligned.
